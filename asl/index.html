Tau Chapter Welcome

Welcome to the home page of the Alpha Sigma Lambda - Tau Chapter at UMUC. This Web site is for both current and prospective members. Prospective members can learn more about the chapter and the honor society through browsing this site (starting at the "About Us" and the "FAQ" sections). Current members can visit this site regularly to view the latest news and to access our communities.

The Tau Chapter of Alpha Sigma Lambda primarily recognizes adult students for their academic excellence through membership. The chapter also helps students reach their academic goals through scholarships and through supporting students by volunteering as mentors and peer tutors.

Tau Chapter Communities

Tau Chapter members are welcome to join our social media community on linked in. This space is where members can network, discuss various topics of interest, share opportunities, and more.
