WindUp Dance Tour

Bethlehem, PA - November 10th, 2019

We are back Bethlehem, PA! Sunday, November 10th. Join us for another incredible all day intensive with a line up of some amazing professionals in the industry - coming to you from Los Angeles and New York City.

Wind Up Dance Tour is an exclusive dance convention that provides comprehensive class initiatives in a variety of dance disciplines. Our goal; to push the dancer out of their comfort zone with unique and challenging classes taught by top choreographers in the industry. We provide the tools; you hold the key to elevating your skills to the next level

Includes: 5 Workshops
  • 1.5 hour workshop with Leighanna Kennett (contemporary)
  • 1.5 hour workshop with Brittany Cherry (ballroom)
  • 1.0 hour long master class with Justin Conte (urban flow)
  • 1.0 hour long master class with Dez Soliven (street jazz)
  • 1.0 hour long master class with Tiadora Rivera (hip hop)
schedule will be released closer to the date via email for registered dancers.
Learn more about our faculty
  • Recommended for ages 10 and up,
  • Intermediate/advanced level dancers.
  • This intensive isn't recommended for beginner dancers
  • Registration begins at 8:30am, Classes start at 9am
  • Registration includes all 5 workshops - $120
Space is LIMITED, we do have a cut off and this will sell out so register your spot early
